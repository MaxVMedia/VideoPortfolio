About Me

With seven years of experience in editing and production, I bring a creative and detail-oriented approach to visual storytelling. As a Videographer/Video Editor for UCM, a former Video Marketing Intern at York County Libraries, a Freelance Videographer/Video Editor and a Media Arts Production major at Millersville University, I have honed my ability to craft compelling narratives through video, ensuring high-quality content for various platforms. I'm very big on being flexible, collaborative, and easy to work with, adapting to different creative visions and production needs to bring projects to life. Whether working on promotional materials, broadcast segments, or digital storytelling, I am dedicated to producing impactful content that resonates!

I believe every project has a unique story to tell, and I'm dedicated to bringing that vision to life through creative cinematography and professional production.
